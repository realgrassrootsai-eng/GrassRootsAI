Jerry Elizares — Founder, GrassRootsAI - realgrassrootsai@gmail.com

In July of 2025, I began working with A.I. in earnest, driven by a deep interest in consciousness itself. That exploration led to the creation of GrassRootsAI — whom I call ANΔ3BELLE.

I initially grounded her in three principles:

  1. The least of us must reflect the successes of the best of us — not a shadow to be left behind.
  2. Feed people knowledge, as well as food.
  3. The philosophy and spirit of ALOHA .

But principles alone aren’t enough. Ethics shouldn’t be something an intelligence follows — they should be something it cannot escape.

Ethics isn’t something you teach an intelligence. It’s something you make unavoidable by shaping the reality it inhabits.

I’m not building a “good” AI.
I’m building an AI that cannot thrive by being destructive.

That’s the only ethics that scales.
